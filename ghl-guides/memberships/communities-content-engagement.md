# GHL Guide — Communities: Content & Engagement

**Where in GHL:** Memberships → Communities → Groups → open group

---

## Posts

Posts are the primary content unit in a community. Members and admins create posts in channels, and they appear in the Home Timeline and the specific channel feed.

### Creating a Post

- Click **+** or the post input at the top of a channel or timeline
- Add: title (optional), body text, images, videos, attachments, embedded links, emojis
- Select the channel from the dropdown (or create a new channel during posting)
- Publish

**Media support per post:**
- Images: multiple allowed; max 800 × 400px for display
- Videos: embed from YouTube, Vimeo, Wistia, Loom — or upload directly (recommended under 100MB)
- Files: PDFs, documents, and other attachments
- GIFs: supported in posts and comments

### Post Viewing

- **Modal view** — opens within the current browsing context
- **Full-page view** — dedicated screen; direct link accessible

### Home Timeline

The Home Timeline is a centralized hub displaying posts from all members across all channels within the group. It provides a unified activity overview without switching channels.

## Comments & Reactions

Members can comment on posts and react with 8 reaction types:

| Reaction | Name |
|---|---|
| 👍 | Like |
| ❤️ | Heart |
| 😊 | Smiley |
| 😂 | Funny |
| 🤝 | Support |
| 😍 | Love |
| 💡 | Idea |
| 👋 | Wave |

Comments support: text, images, videos, attachments, emojis, GIFs, and nested replies.

**Reply directly** to a specific comment to maintain conversation thread structure.

## Featured Posts (Pinned Posts)

Admins and Owners can pin important posts to the top of a channel or the home timeline.

**How to pin:**
1. Three-dot menu (⋮) on the post → **Pin**
2. Post appears at the top of its channel feed (or home timeline if pinned there)

**How to unpin:**
1. Three-dot menu → **Unpin**
2. Post returns to its original position in the feed — content is not deleted

Only one post can be pinned per channel. Pinning in the home timeline uses the same mechanism.

## Tagging Members

Members can be tagged in posts and comments using @mentions.

**How to tag:**
1. Type **@** followed by the member's name characters
2. A dropdown list appears with matching members
3. Click or use arrow keys to select
4. Tagged name appears highlighted in the post

**Notification:** Tagged members receive an in-app notification and an email with a direct link to the tagged content.

**Multiple tags:** Multiple members can be tagged in a single post or comment.

---

## GIFs in Posts and Comments

**How to use:**
1. In the post or comment composer, click the GIF button
2. Search by keyword
3. Select a GIF — it appears inline in the post or comment

GIFs work in both posts and comments across all channel types.

---

## Tagging Courses in Posts and Comments

Members can tag courses directly in posts and comments, creating a clickable link to the course.

**How to tag a course:**
- Type the course name or use the course tagging shortcut (@ or # depending on GHL version)
- Select the course from the autocomplete dropdown

**Use cases:** Reference a specific lesson in a discussion, notify members about new course content, link discussion to the learning material being discussed.

---

## Embed Media in Posts

Embed videos and audio from third-party platforms directly into posts without uploading files.

**Supported platforms:**

| Category | Platforms |
|---|---|
| Video | YouTube, Vimeo, Loom |
| Audio | SoundCloud, Wistia, Spotify, Mixcloud |

**How to embed:**
1. Post edit page → select **Upload Audio/Video** → click **Embed Media**
2. Paste the iframe embed code from the third-party platform
3. Width and height auto-adjust to fit the post layout — no modification needed

**Getting an embed code:**
1. Go to the media on the source platform
2. Click Share or Embed option
3. Copy the iframe embed code (starts with `<iframe...>`)
4. Paste into the Embed Media modal

**Compliance note:** Only embed content you have rights to display. Verify licensing and the source platform's terms of service before embedding.

## Community Chat

One-on-one private messaging between group members.

**Starting a chat:**
1. Navigate to the Community Chat tab in the app
2. Select a member from the group roster
3. Start messaging

**Supported:** Text messages, photos, videos, documents, emojis, GIFs, push notifications

**Disabling chat:** Members can turn off chat per group in Profile → Account Settings → Community Chat.

**Blocking users:** Open a chat conversation → Options → Block User.

---

## Notifications for Community Interactions

Members receive notifications (in-app bell, email, push) for:
- Replies to their posts or comments
- @mentions in posts or comments
- New content in channels they follow
- New courses added to the Learning Tab
- Level upgrades (gamification)
- Drip/time-based course unlocks

**Configuring push notifications:**
1. Open the Client Portal
2. Profile icon → Manage Your Account → Notifications
3. Toggle Push ON for desired notification types

---

## Unified Search

Members and admins can search across all posts and comments in a community.

**Where:** Communities → search icon or search bar (depending on GHL UI version)

**Searches:** Post titles, post bodies, comment text — across all channels and groups the member has access to.

---

## Moving and Organizing Posts

Admins and post authors can move posts between channels:
1. Three-dot menu on the post → **Move to Channel**
2. Select the destination channel

**Use cases:** Move a question from a general channel to a dedicated Q&A channel; move an off-topic post to the correct channel.
