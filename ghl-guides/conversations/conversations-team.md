# GHL Guide — Conversations Team Collaboration

---

## Internal Comments

Private notes added to a conversation thread. Contacts never see them.

**Where:** Conversations → Channel Switcher → Internal Comment

**How to post:**
1. Open a conversation
2. Click the Channel Switcher in the composer
3. Select Internal Comment
4. Type your message — use @ to tag team members
5. Click Send

**Key features:**
- @mentions notify tagged teammates instantly
- Drafts auto-save when switching channels or navigating away
- Searchable via Filters → Internal Comments or Filters → Mentions of Me

**Limitations:**
- Cannot be edited or deleted after posting
- No attachment support
- Notifications depend on individual settings — users should enable mention alerts in Settings → My Profile → Notifications

## Internal Chat

Private messaging between team members only — separate from contact conversations. Contacts never see this.

**Where:** Conversations → My Inbox → Internal Chat folder OR New → Internal Chat

**How to create a chat:**
1. Conversations → New → Internal Chat
2. Select team members
3. Optionally add a subject
4. Send

**How to add users to existing chats:**
- Right panel → Add Users → choose whether new member sees past history or not

**Key rules:**
- Only sub-account users can be added — no external emails
- Only added users can see the conversation — full privacy
- Users cannot be removed once added
- If you "Login As" another user, Internal Chat is hidden — privacy is enforced
- Agency Admins can create chats independently
- No additional cost

**Difference from Internal Comments:**
- Internal Comments: attached to a specific contact's conversation thread
- Internal Chat: standalone team conversation, no contact attached

## Activity Cards

Real-time update cards that appear inside a conversation when key events happen — without you having to navigate away.

**Where:** Appear inline in the message history panel

**Types of Activity Cards:**

**Opportunities**
- Appears when opportunity is created, updated, or deleted
- Click View Opportunity to edit directly inside the card

**Appointments**
- Appears when appointment is created, rescheduled, or cancelled
- Click View Appointment to edit details without leaving Conversations

**Do Not Disturb (DND)**
- Appears when a contact is marked DND or DND is removed
- Shows who made the change: workflow, team member, or the contact themselves
- Shows whether it applies to a specific channel or all channels

**Invoices**
- Appears when an invoice is sent or payment is received
- Click View Invoice to open detailed invoice in a new tab

## @Mentions

Tag a teammate inside an Internal Comment to notify them directly.

**How:** Type @ in the Internal Comment composer → select team member from dropdown

**Notification:** Tagged user receives an alert based on their notification settings

**Find mentions:** Conversations → Filters → Mentions of Me
